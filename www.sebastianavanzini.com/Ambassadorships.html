I WORK WITH BRANDS I BELIEVE IN

Ambassadorships

Sebastian Avanzini, known as "The Brand," proudly represents leading global brands, enhancing his performance both on and off the football field. In addition to ambassadorships, he has modeled for renowned campaigns with brands like Honda, Aston Villa, Puma, and others.

Under Armour

Under Armour is my partner inside and outside the football field. The quality and ambition to always be ready to perform aligns perfectly with my character. Together we continue to push towards our mutual goals and to make me the best athlete I possibly can be.

Wardrobe

Being half Italian, it is no surprise that minimalism, elegance & quality are my main concerns when it comes to fashion. With Wardrobe I have the perfect partner. Being a local brand brand, and one of the leading high-end fashion brands in South East Asia, Wardrobe and I continue to redefine the perception of how athletes and fashion go hand in hand.

Optimum Nutrition

Optimum Nutrition has been fueling my journey, ensuring I have the right supplements to always push my limits. When living a performance-based lifestyle with multiple trainings every day, nutrition and having the right supplements becomes crucial.
