Ambassadorships

As an ambassador, Sebastian Avanzini is passionate about education and aspires to create equal opportunities for all children, helping build brighter futures.

At Educate Bali.com, our mission is to ensure every child across Bali & South East Asia has access to quality English education, equipping them with the skills to succeed in a globalized world. We partner with schools, provide a network of qualified teachers, and distribute educational resources to even the most underserved areas. We believe that, every child deserves the chance to succeed and become a global citizen. Through community clubs and cultural events, we engage locals and tourists to raise awareness and support. Our goal is simple: to create a brighter future for Bali, one child at a time. Together, we can make a lasting impact on the children of South East Asia.
