The Athlete

I’ve been a football player since before I could walk, always with a ball at my feet. Despite a very unorthodox path to the top, I never hesitated in fighting for and following my dream. Football has always been my passion, and it continues to drive me every day. Along the way, I’ve faced challenges that have shaped me, but the commitment to my craft has never wavered. Each step has brought me closer to where I am today, and my journey is far from over.

The Dream

A boy from a small town, with big dreams. My biggest ambition as a young kid was to captain my local team, a dream I accomplished by the age of 18. Once that goal was fulfilled, I set my sights on a professional football career, reaching the highest level in Denmark. From there, I moved abroad, embracing new cultures and challenges as a professional football player. But it all started when I was too young to even be registered at a football club, and my dad would drop me off with kids 2-3 years older than me. With that same enthusiasm as the kid who started with nothing but passion and big dreams, I continue pushing forward, knowing that there are still many more dreams to fulfill.

Kuala Lumpur City F.C

I played the majority of my professional career around Europe. So much happiness and joy, so many successes, some tough times, a lot of unforgettable nights and lots of great friendships along the way. I left when I was 27. Saying goodbye to my family wasn’t easy, but to grow and develop as a human and athlete, I needed a new challenge.

Europe

My arrival in Kuala Lumpur City F.C was unexpected. A great club, an ambitious project and a spectacular city. A completely different challenge in a very different culture with a huge love for football. I enjoyed an extremely successfull year with KL City, reaching the FA Cup Final. There were also difficult moments that, as ever, help you grow and learn on a personal and sporting level. In Kuala Lumpur I’ll always have a home.

Negeri Sembilan F.C

Having established my name in Asian football, it was time to embrace a new challenge. I joined the historical club Negeri Sembilan F.C with the ambition to add a new exciting chapter to my journey. It is truly an honour to be part of a great club like NSFC.
