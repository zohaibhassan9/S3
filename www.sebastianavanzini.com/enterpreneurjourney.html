The Entrepreneur

I have always been a visionary. Driving change and creating new solutions that can inspire and help, has always been a part of my character. My entrepreneurial journey has taken me through many different fields. Currently, I am the co-founder of two tech-companies, where my mission is to redefine the perception and way we think of education.

Eduvo.io

Eduvo.io is an educational platform that allows anybody to be mentored and taught by some of the World’s leading experts and online creators. By partnering up with thought leaders and industry experts, we specialize in creating in-depth mentorships & educations, providing detailed blueprints and insights on how to succeed in any field, by breaking it down into 3-6 month long online educations.
This has lead to recognition all the way from Silicon Valley to London, emphasizing our contributions to education on a global level.

SocialDay.io

SocialDay.io is the new kid in class. A concept with the purpose of matchmaking companies with the right commercial talent for their campaigns. In a world that is becoming more and more automatised, we strive to create the World’s leading platform, connecting corporations with influential creators and their audiences to ensure brand alignment for future collaborations.
